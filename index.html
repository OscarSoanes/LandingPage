Welcome to Rabbit House

Chino the granddaughter of the Rabbit House's owner. A soft-spoken girl who slowly warms up to Cocoa, though is often annoyed by her insistence on being called "big sister. She has a reserved and emotionless disposition, rarely smiling even when happy, and she speaks in a flat monotone voice with polite grammar. She takes her work very seriously and gets annoyed when people act recklessly or loudly around her."
